Pound Cake

Lartashia Taylor

ingredients:

  • 1 Cup (2 Sticks) unsalted butter
  • 2 Cups Granulated sugar
  • 4 Large Eggs
  • 2 Teaspoons Vanilla
  • 3 Cups All purpose flour
  • 2 Teaspoon bakingpowder
  • 1/2 baking soda
  • 1 Cup sour cream

Instruction:

  1. Preheat oven 325F>
  2. Grease Cake Pan
  3. In a alarge bowl , cream the softened Butter
  4. Granulated sugar until light and fluffy
  5. Add the Eggsone at time , beating well
  6. Vanilla in another bowl whisk flour, baking soda, bakingpowder
  7. Add sour cream
  8. Pour all ingredients in cake pan
  9. Bake for 60-70 minutes

This is a elevate dessert with a delicious homemade taste that will knock your socks off
